Armante Welch

Armante Welch is known for Thieves (2017), Ketchup and Blood (2016) and Choices(2017). Born August 12, 1987 in Chicago,IL. Grew up in Cicero,IL to Charles and Natalie Welch. He is the second oldest child of his family.He went to Morton West Highschool and was a theater major at Southern Illinois University. His first television appearance was "Cold Case Files:Chicago." He also was featured on "Brooklyn SIU". In 2019 he booked a video game commercial for Pen and Teller. In 2023, he was featured a national commercial "Protect" for Bio-Solutions

He's currently an intern working as a consultant and developer for I.C.Stars. He's developing an app for a non-profit organization Thresholds. The program is an internship that teaches business and technolgy skills. He was chosen amongst 21 interns out of 500.

After commencement, he plans to pursue a career in web development with hopes of being an IOS developer for apple.

Armante now lives in Dyer, IN. He plans to move back to Chicago, and give back to non-profit organizations and invest in his acting career.You can find him on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.
